REJOICE

A Special Journey from Friendship to Something More

I still remember the day we first talked—it felt so natural, like we had known each other forever. What started as simple conversations, laughter, and shared moments soon became something I truly cherish.

Over time, I realized that you weren’t just a friend—you were someone who made my days brighter, someone I looked forward to talking to, and someone who unknowingly became a big part of my life.

Lately, I’ve noticed that the way I feel about you has changed. It’s no longer just friendship—it’s something deeper, something real. I find myself thinking about you more, wanting to be there for you in ways I never thought about before.

I don’t know where this journey will take us, but I do know one thing—I want to walk this path with you. No rush, no pressure, just two hearts figuring out something beautiful together.

From Friendship to Forever

We met like a whisper, a soft summer breeze,

Two wandering souls, at ease, at peace.

With laughter as light as the morning sky,

And words that danced, no need to try.

You were the smile on a cloudy day,

A quiet warmth that chose to stay.

Through endless talks and moments small,

I never knew I'd start to fall.

At first, just a friend, a hand to hold,

A heart so gentle, yet pure and bold.

But love, like seasons, slow and true,

Began to bloom where friendship grew.

I found you in sunsets, in songs unsung,

In late-night talks and air in my lungs.

A presence so steady, a comfort so bright,

A star that glowed in my darkest night.

Now here we stand, unsure yet strong,

A melody waiting to be a song.

No rush, no race, just hearts aware,

That something special lingers there.

So if this is love, let’s take it slow,

Let’s let the winds of wonder blow.

For you, my friend, are more than you see,

You are the best kind of love to me.
